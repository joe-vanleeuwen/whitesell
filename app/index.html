Natalee Whitesell

Writer, Speaker, Mentor, Advocate For The Mentally Ill

I suffer with bi-polar type 1. I am coping with and healing from this disease. I am in the process of not being defined by it. I desire to bring mental illness into the Light, where there is healing and restoration.

I live in Greenville, SC with my husband and four kiddos. I've authored a short book, called Dismantling Taboo. It's my story of my battle with mental illness, and of Hope. I speak to churches and or organizations about how they can help the mentally ill.

I write on my blog from time to time: PracticePresence.org
